## Los Colores

### Basic Colors (Colores Básicos)

- **Rojo** - Red
- **Azul** - Blue
- **Amarillo** - Yellow
- **Verde** - Green
- **Naranja** - Orange
- **Morado / Púrpura** - Purple
- **Rosa / Rosado** - Pink
- **Marrón / Café** - Brown
- **Negro** - Black
- **Blanco** - White
- **Gris** - Gray

### Shades and Variations (Tonos y Variaciones)

**Light and Dark:**
- **Claro** - Light
- **Oscuro** - Dark
- **Azul claro** - Light blue
- **Azul oscuro** - Dark blue
- **Verde claro** - Light green
- **Verde oscuro** - Dark green

**Metallic:**
- **Dorado** - Golden
- **Plateado** - Silver
- **Bronce** - Bronze

**Other Shades:**
- **Beige** - Beige
- **Turquesa** - Turquoise
- **Violeta** - Violet
- **Celeste** - Sky blue
- **Crema** - Cream
- **Carmesí** - Crimson
- **Magenta** - Magenta

### Grammar with Colors

**Agreement:**
Most colors must agree in gender and number with the noun:

- El coche **rojo** - the red car (masculine singular)
- La casa **roja** - the red house (feminine singular)
- Los coches **rojos** - the red cars (masculine plural)
- Las casas **rojas** - the red houses (feminine plural)

**Invariable Colors:**
Some colors don't change:
- **Naranja** - orange
- **Rosa** - pink
- **Violeta** - violet
- **Turquesa** - turquoise

Examples:
- El vestido **naranja** - the orange dress
- Los vestidos **naranja** - the orange dresses
- La camisa **rosa** - the pink shirt
- Las camisas **rosa** - the pink shirts

### Asking About Colors

- **¿De qué color es?** - What color is it?
- **¿Cuál es tu color favorito?** - What's your favorite color?
- **Mi color favorito es el azul** - My favorite color is blue

### Common Expressions

**Describing Objects:**
- El cielo es **azul** - The sky is blue
- La hierba es **verde** - The grass is green
- El sol es **amarillo** - The sun is yellow
- La nieve es **blanca** - The snow is white
- La noche es **negra** - The night is black

**Clothing:**
- Llevo una camisa **blanca** - I'm wearing a white shirt
- Tengo zapatos **negros** - I have black shoes
- Quiero la falda **roja** - I want the red skirt

**Common Objects:**
- Un coche **azul** - a blue car
- Una casa **amarilla** - a yellow house
- Flores **rojas** - red flowers
- Ojos **verdes** - green eyes
- Pelo **negro** - black hair
- Pelo **rubio** - blonde hair
- Pelo **castaño** - brown hair

### Idiomatic Expressions

- **Ponerse rojo** - to blush (literally: to turn red)
- **Estar verde** - to be inexperienced (literally: to be green)
- **Príncipe azul** - Prince Charming (literally: blue prince)
- **Noche en blanco** - sleepless night (literally: white night)
- **Estar negro** - to be angry (literally: to be black)
- **Verse negro** - to have a hard time
- **Chiste verde** - dirty joke (literally: green joke)
