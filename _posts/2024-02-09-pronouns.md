## Los Pronombres

### Subject Pronouns (Pronombres Personales)

Used as the subject of a sentence:

**Singular:**
- **Yo** - I
- **Tú** - You (informal)
- **Él** - He
- **Ella** - She
- **Usted** - You (formal)

**Plural:**
- **Nosotros/Nosotras** - We
- **Vosotros/Vosotras** - You all (informal, Spain)
- **Ellos/Ellas** - They
- **Ustedes** - You all (formal, or all in Latin America)

**Examples:**
- **Yo** hablo español - I speak Spanish
- **Tú** comes pizza - You eat pizza
- **Nosotros** vivimos aquí - We live here

### Reflexive Pronouns (Pronombres Reflexivos)

Used with reflexive verbs (actions done to oneself):

- **me** - myself
- **te** - yourself (informal)
- **se** - himself, herself, yourself (formal), themselves
- **nos** - ourselves
- **os** - yourselves (informal, Spain)

**Examples:**
- **Me** lavo las manos - I wash my hands
- **Te** levantas temprano - You get up early
- **Se** llama María - Her name is María
- **Nos** duchamos - We shower
- **Se** visten rápido - They get dressed quickly

### Possessive Pronouns (Pronombres Posesivos)

Replace nouns to show ownership:

**Singular:**
- **mío/mía** - mine
- **tuyo/tuya** - yours (informal)
- **suyo/suya** - his, hers, yours (formal)

**Plural:**
- **míos/mías** - mine
- **tuyos/tuyas** - yours (informal)
- **suyos/suyas** - his, hers, yours (formal)
- **nuestro/nuestra/nuestros/nuestras** - ours
- **vuestro/vuestra/vuestros/vuestras** - yours (informal, Spain)

**Examples:**
- Este libro es **mío** - This book is mine
- La casa es **tuya** - The house is yours
- Los coches son **nuestros** - The cars are ours
- ¿Es **suya** esta maleta? - Is this suitcase yours/his/hers?

### Demonstrative Pronouns (Pronombres Demostrativos)

Point to specific things:

**Near (this, these):**
- **éste/ésta/éstos/éstas** - this one, these ones
- **esto** - this (neuter)

**Medium (that, those):**
- **ése/ésa/ésos/ésas** - that one, those ones
- **eso** - that (neuter)

**Far (that over there):**
- **aquél/aquélla/aquéllos/aquéllas** - that one over there
- **aquello** - that over there (neuter)

**Examples:**
- Prefiero **éste** - I prefer this one
- **Ése** es mejor - That one is better
- **Aquél** es mi hermano - That one over there is my brother
- ¿Qué es **esto**? - What is this?

### Relative Pronouns (Pronombres Relativos)

Connect clauses:

- **que** - that, which, who
- **quien/quienes** - who, whom
- **el/la/los/las cual(es)** - which, who
- **cuyo/cuya/cuyos/cuyas** - whose
- **lo que** - what, that which

**Examples:**
- El libro **que** leo - The book that I read
- La persona **que** vino - The person who came
- El hombre **con quien** hablo - The man with whom I speak
- La casa **cuyo** dueño conozco - The house whose owner I know
- **Lo que** dices es verdad - What you say is true

### Interrogative Pronouns (Pronombres Interrogativos)

Ask questions:

- **¿Qué?** - What?
- **¿Quién/Quiénes?** - Who?
- **¿Cuál/Cuáles?** - Which one(s)?
- **¿Cuánto/Cuánta/Cuántos/Cuántas?** - How much/many?
- **¿Dónde?** - Where?
- **¿Cuándo?** - When?
- **¿Cómo?** - How?
- **¿Por qué?** - Why?

**Examples:**
- **¿Qué** quieres? - What do you want?
- **¿Quién** es? - Who is it?
- **¿Cuál** prefieres? - Which one do you prefer?
- **¿Cuántos** años tienes? - How many years do you have?

### Prepositional Pronouns (Pronombres Preposicionales)

Used after prepositions:

- **mí** - me
- **ti** - you (informal)
- **él/ella/usted** - him, her, you (formal)
- **nosotros/nosotras** - us
- **vosotros/vosotras** - you all (informal, Spain)
- **ellos/ellas/ustedes** - them, you all

**Special forms with "con" (with):**
- **conmigo** - with me
- **contigo** - with you
- **consigo** - with himself/herself/yourself

**Examples:**
- Para **mí** - For me
- Sin **ti** - Without you
- Con **él** - With him
- Voy **contigo** - I'm going with you
