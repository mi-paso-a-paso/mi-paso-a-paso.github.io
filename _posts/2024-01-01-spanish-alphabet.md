## El Alfabeto Español

The Spanish alphabet has 27 letters. Let's learn each one with its pronunciation.

### The Letters

- **A** (a) - as in "father"
- **B** (be) - like English "b"
- **C** (ce) - like "k" before a, o, u; like "s" before e, i
- **D** (de) - like English "d"
- **E** (e) - as in "met"
- **F** (efe) - like English "f"
- **G** (ge) - like "g" in "go" before a, o, u; like "h" before e, i
- **H** (hache) - silent in Spanish
- **I** (i) - as in "meet"
- **J** (jota) - like English "h" in "hello"
- **K** (ka) - like English "k"
- **L** (ele) - like English "l"
- **M** (eme) - like English "m"
- **N** (ene) - like English "n"
- **Ñ** (eñe) - like "ny" in "canyon"
- **O** (o) - as in "hope"
- **P** (pe) - like English "p"
- **Q** (cu) - like English "k", always followed by "ue" or "ui"
- **R** (ere) - rolled "r"
- **S** (ese) - like English "s"
- **T** (te) - like English "t"
- **U** (u) - as in "moon"
- **V** (uve) - like English "v" or soft "b"
- **W** (uve doble) - like English "w"
- **X** (equis) - like English "x"
- **Y** (ye or i griega) - like English "y"
- **Z** (zeta) - like English "s" or "th" in Spain

### Practice Words

- **Casa** (house)
- **Niño** (boy)
- **Jaula** (cage)
- **Queso** (cheese)
- **Zapato** (shoe)
