## Vocabulario de Direcciones

### Basic Directions (Direcciones Básicas)

**Cardinal Directions:**
- **Norte** - North
- **Sur** - South
- **Este** - East
- **Oeste** - West
- **Noreste** - Northeast
- **Noroeste** - Northwest
- **Sureste** - Southeast
- **Suroeste** - Southwest

### Positional Words

**Left and Right:**
- **Izquierda** - Left
- **Derecha** - Right
- **A la izquierda** - To the left / On the left
- **A la derecha** - To the right / On the right

**Up and Down:**
- **Arriba** - Up / Upstairs
- **Abajo** - Down / Downstairs
- **Hacia arriba** - Upward
- **Hacia abajo** - Downward

### Location Words

**Here, There, Over There:**
- **Aquí** - Here
- **Ahí** - There (near you)
- **Allí / Allá** - There / Over there (far from both)
- **Acá** - Here (less specific than aquí)

**Examples:**
- Estoy **aquí** - I'm here
- Pon el libro **ahí** - Put the book there
- La tienda está **allí** - The store is over there

### Prepositions of Place

**Basic Prepositions:**
- **En** - In / On / At
- **Sobre** - On / Over
- **Bajo** - Under
- **Debajo de** - Under / Beneath
- **Encima de** - On top of / Above
- **Dentro de** - Inside
- **Fuera de** - Outside
- **Delante de** - In front of
- **Detrás de** - Behind
- **Al lado de** - Next to / Beside
- **Cerca de** - Near / Close to
- **Lejos de** - Far from
- **Entre** - Between / Among
- **Enfrente de** - In front of / Opposite
- **Junto a** - Next to
- **Frente a** - Facing / In front of

**Examples:**
- El libro está **sobre** la mesa - The book is on the table
- El perro está **debajo de** la cama - The dog is under the bed
- La farmacia está **al lado de** la tienda - The pharmacy is next to the store
- El banco está **enfrente de** la plaza - The bank is in front of the square

### Movement Directions

**Verbs:**
- **Ir** - To go
- **Venir** - To come
- **Subir** - To go up / To climb
- **Bajar** - To go down / To descend
- **Entrar** - To enter
- **Salir** - To exit / To leave
- **Cruzar** - To cross
- **Seguir** - To follow / To continue
- **Girar** - To turn
- **Doblar** - To turn
- **Caminar** - To walk
- **Pasar** - To pass

**Commands for Directions:**
- **Siga recto** - Go straight
- **Gire a la izquierda** - Turn left
- **Gire a la derecha** - Turn right
- **Doble en la esquina** - Turn at the corner
- **Cruce la calle** - Cross the street
- **Suba las escaleras** - Go up the stairs
- **Baje por esta calle** - Go down this street
- **Tome la primera calle** - Take the first street
- **Pase el semáforo** - Pass the traffic light
- **Continúe recto** - Continue straight

### Distance and Location

**Distance:**
- **Cerca** - Close / Near
- **Lejos** - Far
- **Muy cerca** - Very close
- **Muy lejos** - Very far
- **A la vuelta** - Around the corner
- **A dos cuadras** - Two blocks away
- **A cinco minutos** - Five minutes away
- **A un kilómetro** - One kilometer away

**Position:**
- **En la esquina** - On the corner
- **Al final de la calle** - At the end of the street
- **En el centro** - In the center
- **En la primera planta** - On the first floor
- **En el segundo piso** - On the second floor
- **Al fondo** - At the back / In the back

### Places and Landmarks

**Common Places:**
- **La calle** - The street
- **La avenida** - The avenue
- **La plaza** - The square
- **El parque** - The park
- **El puente** - The bridge
- **La rotonda** - The roundabout
- **El semáforo** - The traffic light
- **La señal** - The sign
- **La esquina** - The corner
- **El cruce** - The crossing / Intersection
- **La intersección** - The intersection

### Complete Direction Phrases

**Giving Directions:**
- **Está a la derecha del banco** - It's to the right of the bank
- **Está enfrente de la iglesia** - It's in front of the church
- **Está al lado del supermercado** - It's next to the supermarket
- **Está entre la farmacia y el café** - It's between the pharmacy and the café
- **Está detrás del hotel** - It's behind the hotel
- **Está cerca de aquí** - It's close to here
- **Está lejos de aquí** - It's far from here

**Example Directions:**
1. Siga recto por esta calle - Go straight down this street
2. Gire a la izquierda en el semáforo - Turn left at the traffic light
3. Continúe dos cuadras - Continue two blocks
4. La tienda está a la derecha - The store is on the right
5. Está enfrente del parque - It's in front of the park

### Useful Questions

- **¿Dónde está...?** - Where is...?
- **¿Cómo llego a...?** - How do I get to...?
- **¿Está cerca?** - Is it close?
- **¿Está lejos?** - Is it far?
- **¿Cuánto tiempo se tarda?** - How long does it take?
- **¿Por dónde se va?** - Which way do you go?
- **¿En qué dirección?** - In which direction?
