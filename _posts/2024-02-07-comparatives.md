## Los Comparativos

Comparatives are used to compare two things or people.

### Comparative of Superiority (más... que)

**Structure: más + adjective/adverb + que**

**Examples:**
- Juan es **más alto que** Pedro - Juan is taller than Pedro
- Este libro es **más interesante que** ese - This book is more interesting than that one
- María corre **más rápido que** su hermana - María runs faster than her sister
- El coche es **más caro que** la bicicleta - The car is more expensive than the bicycle

### Comparative of Inferiority (menos... que)

**Structure: menos + adjective/adverb + que**

**Examples:**
- Pedro es **menos alto que** Juan - Pedro is less tall than Juan
- Esta película es **menos aburrida que** la otra - This movie is less boring than the other one
- Hoy hace **menos frío que** ayer - Today it's less cold than yesterday
- El tren es **menos rápido que** el avión - The train is less fast than the plane

### Comparative of Equality (tan... como)

**Structure: tan + adjective/adverb + como**

**Examples:**
- Ana es **tan inteligente como** Luis - Ana is as intelligent as Luis
- Mi casa es **tan grande como** la tuya - My house is as big as yours
- Él habla **tan bien como** tú - He speaks as well as you
- Este restaurante es **tan bueno como** el otro - This restaurant is as good as the other one

### Equality with Nouns (tanto... como)

**Structure: tanto/a/os/as + noun + como**

**Examples:**
- Tengo **tantos libros como** tú - I have as many books as you
- Hay **tanta gente como** ayer - There are as many people as yesterday
- Bebo **tanto café como** mi hermano - I drink as much coffee as my brother
- Tiene **tantas amigas como** yo - She has as many friends as I do

### Irregular Comparatives

**Bueno (good):**
- Más bueno → **mejor** (better)
- Este vino es **mejor que** ese - This wine is better than that one

**Malo (bad):**
- Más malo → **peor** (worse)
- Esta película es **peor que** la otra - This movie is worse than the other one

**Grande (big) - for age:**
- Más grande → **mayor** (older/bigger)
- Mi hermano es **mayor que** yo - My brother is older than me

**Pequeño (small) - for age:**
- Más pequeño → **menor** (younger/smaller)
- Ella es **menor que** su prima - She is younger than her cousin

### Superlatives

**Relative Superlative:**
- El/La/Los/Las **más/menos** + adjective + **de**
- Juan es **el más alto de** la clase - Juan is the tallest in the class
- Esta es **la película más interesante del** año - This is the most interesting movie of the year

**Absolute Superlative (-ísimo):**
- Adjective + **-ísimo/a/os/as**
- La comida está **riquísima** - The food is extremely delicious
- Es **facilísimo** - It's very easy
- Estoy **cansadísimo** - I'm extremely tired
