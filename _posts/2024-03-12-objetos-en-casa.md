### Common Expressions

- **¿Dónde está el control remoto?** - Where is the remote control?
- **Necesito la escoba** - I need the broom
- **Busco mis llaves** - I'm looking for my keys
- **La lámpara no funciona** - The lamp doesn't work
- **Voy a lavar los platos** - I'm going to wash the dishes
- **Tengo que planchar la ropa** - I have to iron the clothes
- **¿Hay papel higiénico?** - Is there toilet paper?
- **Pon las flores en el jarrón** - Put the flowers in the vase

### Actions with Objects (Acciones con Objetos)

- **Usar** - to use
- **Guardar** - to put away/store
- **Sacar** - to take out
- **Limpiar** - to clean
- **Lavar** - to wash
- **Secar** - to dry
- **Ordenar** - to organize/tidy up
- **Colocar** - to place/put
- **Mover** - to move
- **Encender** - to turn on
- **Apagar** - to turn off
- **Conectar** - to connect/plug in
- **Desconectar** - to disconnect/unplug
