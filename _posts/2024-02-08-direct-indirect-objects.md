## Objetos Directos e Indirectos

### Direct Object (Objeto Directo)

The direct object receives the action of the verb directly. It answers "what?" or "whom?"

**Direct Object Pronouns:**
- **me** - me
- **te** - you (informal)
- **lo** - him, it (masculine), you (formal masculine)
- **la** - her, it (feminine), you (formal feminine)
- **nos** - us
- **os** - you all (informal, Spain)
- **los** - them (masculine), you all (formal masculine)
- **las** - them (feminine), you all (formal feminine)

**Position:** Before the conjugated verb or attached to infinitive/gerund

**Examples:**
- Veo a Juan → **Lo** veo - I see him
- Compro el libro → **Lo** compro - I buy it
- Quiero comprar**lo** - I want to buy it
- Estoy leyendo**lo** - I'm reading it
- **Te** quiero - I love you
- **La** conozco bien - I know her well
- **Los** veo mañana - I'll see them tomorrow

### Indirect Object (Objeto Indirecto)

The indirect object indicates to whom or for whom the action is performed. It answers "to whom?" or "for whom?"

**Indirect Object Pronouns:**
- **me** - to/for me
- **te** - to/for you (informal)
- **le** - to/for him, her, you (formal)
- **nos** - to/for us
- **os** - to/for you all (informal, Spain)
- **les** - to/for them, you all (formal)

**Position:** Same as direct object pronouns

**Examples:**
- Doy un regalo a María → **Le** doy un regalo - I give her a gift
- Escribo una carta a mis padres → **Les** escribo una carta - I write them a letter
- **Me** gusta el café - I like coffee (literally: Coffee is pleasing to me)
- **Te** digo la verdad - I tell you the truth
- **Nos** compra flores - He/She buys us flowers

### Using Both Together

When using both pronouns together:
1. Indirect object comes first
2. Direct object comes second
3. **le/les** changes to **se** before lo/la/los/las

**Structure: se + lo/la/los/las**

**Examples:**
- Doy el libro a Juan → **Se lo** doy - I give it to him
- Compro flores para María → **Se las** compro - I buy them for her
- Escribo una carta a ellos → **Se la** escribo - I write it to them
- Envío el paquete a ti → **Te lo** envío - I send it to you
- Explico la lección a nosotros → **Nos la** explica - He/She explains it to us

### Clarification

To clarify **se**, add **a + pronoun/noun:**

- **Se lo** doy **a él** - I give it to him
- **Se lo** doy **a ella** - I give it to her
- **Se lo** doy **a usted** - I give it to you (formal)
- **Se los** mando **a ellos** - I send them to them

### Common Verbs with Indirect Objects

- **Dar** (to give): Le doy un regalo
- **Decir** (to say): Te digo la verdad
- **Prestar** (to lend): Me prestas el libro
- **Mostrar** (to show): Les muestro la casa
- **Enviar** (to send): Nos envían cartas
- **Pedir** (to ask for): Le pido ayuda
- **Explicar** (to explain): Te explico el problema
- **Gustar** (to like): Me gusta la música

### Practice Examples

- Veo a María → **La** veo
- Escribo a Juan → **Le** escribo
- Escribo una carta a Juan → **Se la** escribo
- Doy el libro a ti → **Te lo** doy
- Compramos regalos para ellos → **Se los** compramos
